Teleport Map is a mod inspired by the tshock plugin MapTP(github.com/Rozen4334/MapTP).

Simply craft the GlobalTeleporter and put it in the inventory.
Open the fullmap and rightclick while pressing leftshift.
You could teleport to the position your mouse targeted if you have the GlobalTeleporter in your inventory.

GlobalTeleporter could be craft using 999*5 DirtBlock,
                                      999*5 StoneBlock,
                                      999*5 MudBlock,
                                      999*5 SandBlock,
                                      99 *5 Dynamite.
